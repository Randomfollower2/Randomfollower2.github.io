Absurdist Reddit Bot

For this project, I didn't support a specific candidate. Instead, I wanted to create an Reddit bot that undermines the currrent administration through absurdist (fake) claims. The claims are generally about Donald Trump, Mike Pence, the Republican Party, and other characters in the current administration.

In the example below, my bot gives a scathing reply to another bot that was supporting Donald Trump. My appreciation of this thread directly corresponds with its stupidity...
